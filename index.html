Everything you need

Finance management,
beautifully simplified

Every feature you need to take control of your personal finances, with none of the complexity.

Budget Planning

Set planned amounts for income, expenses, bills, savings, and debt. Track actual vs planned with instant feedback.

Transaction Ledger

Log every transaction, categorize spending, and filter by type, date, or description to understand where your money goes.

Bills & Due Dates

Track recurring bills with due dates. Overdue bills are highlighted so you never miss a payment deadline again.

Visual Charts

Donut charts, allocation bars, and planned vs actual comparisons make your budget data instantly understandable.

Encrypted Backups

Export your budget data with optional AES-GCM encryption. Import it back on any device — your passphrase is never stored.

Smart Insights

Budget health score, utilization rates, and largest expense tracking give you clear signals about your financial habits.

Your data never leaves your device

FinanceOS stores everything in your browser's localStorage. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your financial data stays entirely private. Encrypted backups let you safely move data between devices on your own terms.
